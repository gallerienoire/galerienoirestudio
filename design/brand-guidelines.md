# Galerie Noire — Brand Guidelines

*Version 1.0 | June 2026*

---

## 1. Brand Essence

> *We don't decorate rooms. We curate living spaces around art made for you.*

Galerie Noire is a luxury interior-design service that commissions original digital artworks tailored to a client's specific space — and then designs the entire room around those pieces. We are a gallery and a design studio in one.

### Brand Personality
- **Refined** — nothing excessive, everything intentional
- **Moody** — atmospheric, cinematic, emotionally rich
- **Curatorial** — gallery-trained eye, museum quality
- **Personal** — bespoke, one-of-a-kind, made for you
- **Timeless** — beyond trends, built to last

### Brand Voice
- Sophisticated but not stiff
- Warm but not casual
- Authoritative but not pretentious
- Speaks like a gallerist or an editor, not a salesperson

## 2. Logo

### Wordmark
The primary logo is "Galerie Noire" set in an elegant serif typeface (Playfair Display or similar). The wordmark is always presented on a dark background — never on white.

- `logo-wordmark.png` — Full wordmark + optional icon, dark background, warm gold text
- `logo-icon.png` — Standalone icon (abstract diamond/frame with fine art line drawing)

### Clear Space
Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "G" on all sides.

### Minimum Size
- Wordmark: never smaller than 120px wide in digital
- Icon: never smaller than 32px

### Incorrect Usage
- Never place on a light/white background
- Never change the color from brass gold (#C5A55A) on dark
- Never add effects (drop shadows, gradients, glows)
- Never rotate or distort

## 4. Typography

### Headings: Playfair Display
A refined, transitional serif with dramatic thick-thin contrast. Conveys luxury, tradition, and editorial authority.

```
Playfair Display
ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ
abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz
0123456789 !?&@

Weights: Bold (700), Semi-Bold (600), Regular (400)
Letter-spacing: -0.02em (headings), 0.05em (all-caps)
```

### Body: Inter
A clean, highly readable sans-serif with excellent legibility at all sizes.

```
Inter
ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ

0123456789 !?&@

Weights: Regular (400), Medium (500), Light (300)
Line-height: 1.6 (body), 1.3 (small headings)
```

## 5. Visual Language

### Photography Style
- **Moody, low-key lighting** — deep shadows, single light source
- **Warm tones** — brass, amber, candlelight highlights
- **Architecture-forward** — wide angles, leading lines, negative space
- **Art-centric** — every shot centers or frames an artwork
- **Editorial** — composed like Architectural Digest or Cabana Magazine

### Texture & Materials
- Charcoal linen, raw silk, brushed brass, blackened steel
- Honed marble, limestone, dark oak
- Velvet in deep green and charcoal
- Matte finishes preferred over glossy

### Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace — margins of 120px+ on large screens
- Content width capped at 1200px
- Full-bleed hero sections with overlaid text
- Asymmetric layouts for visual interest
- Thin brass gold divider lines (1px) between sections

### Animation & Motion
- Subtle, slow, and deliberate — nothing fast or bouncy
- Fade-in transitions on scroll (0.6s ease)
- Hover effects: gentle opacity shifts, no scaling or bouncing
- Page transitions: smooth crossfade

## 8. Application Guidelines

### Website
- Dark mode by default — charcoal (#1A1A1A) background throughout
- Cream (#F5F0EB) for primary text
- Brass Gold (#C5A55A) for CTAs, hover states, decorative accents
- Forest Green (#1E3A2F) for secondary sections, cards
- Full-bleed imagery with text overlays on hero sections
- Serif headings, sans-serif body

### Social Media
- Profile photo: Icon mark on charcoal
- Cover images: Full-bleed mood photography with subtle logo overlay
- Posts: Dark backgrounds, large typography, generous whitespace
- No bright colors, no gradients, no emoji in brand content

### Print
- Matte paper stock, preferably textured (linen or felt finish)
- Brass foil stamping for logo on covers
- Dark covers with cream interior pages
- Generous margins (minimum 0.75in)
